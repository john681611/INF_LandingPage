About Iron-Fists

Iron-Fists is a UK based clan that takes a semi-pro approach to operations. We like a challenge and often play way out numbered and gunned. We use ACE3, 3CB, RHS, CUP Maps and TFAR. We run three members nights a week and two public servers.

Weekly Member Events

All events start at 9pm UK Time we ask you try and be present at 8:30.

  • Tuesday: Free Night - Vote on the forums of what you want to do.
  • Thursday: Training Night - Learn new skills and practice them. Usually ending in a Zeus mission.
  • Sunday: Mission Night - Take on a serious mission

Leadership Structure

We run a three man command for day to day running:
INF_H@wKEYE - Squad CO
INF_Rhys - 2IC
INF_Mearcat - 3IC
All Major Decisions are put to a squad vote.
All members are listened to rank is only used for tie breakers and mission command structure.

Advancement

Promotions are given for dedication and squad work. Awards are given for completing training courses and actions.

Requirements

We ask that you are:

  • 17 or older
  • Can speak and write in English.
  • Attend member events.
  • Keep up-to-date with squad activities.
  • Are not a member of another Arma Clan.
